About our
5KG gold bars

Investment Case

Why invest in
5KG of gold?

A 5KG gold position is not a casual purchase. It is a deliberate allocation, made by investors who understand that physical gold offers something paper assets cannot: a store of value that exists independently of any bank, currency, or financial system.

The institutional data supports the case for large-volume gold acquisition. Central banks globally purchased more than 1,000 tonnes of gold annually in 2022, 2023, and 2024, almost double the rate of the previous decade. In Q2 2025 alone, they added a further 166 tonnes to their reserves. These purchases reflect a deliberate strategy of diversification away from dollar-denominated assets, driven by both macroeconomic conditions and geopolitical considerations. When the world's most sophisticated financial institutions are building gold reserves at this scale, the case for physical gold as a long-term wealth-preservation vehicle is backed by institutional behaviour.

For buyers starting with a smaller initial position, our 1kg gold bars offer the same mine-direct sourcing, the same assay certification, and the same export documentation process. The 5KG bar is the choice for investors ready to build a position that makes a material difference to a portfolio.
