About This Product

What are
gold nuggets?

Gold nuggets are pieces of naturally occurring gold found in alluvial deposits and hard rock formations, recovered before any smelting or refining takes place. This is a distinct product from a doré bar, which is smelted from ore at the mine, or a refined investment bar, which is processed to a defined purity standard. Each nugget is individually formed, variable in weight, and traceable to a specific geological origin.

Purity in a naturally occurring nugget is not a fixed specification. It varies by deposit and by lot, unlike a refined bar grade which meets a standard definition. Bushiangala's nuggets are independently assayed per lot, and the verified purity figure is confirmed for the buyer before the transaction proceeds. Private investors value the direct mine-to-buyer chain. Collectors value the provenance and the natural character of the piece.

Provenance & Origin

Kakamega County, Kenya

Kakamega County in western Kenya is one of the country's primary gold-producing regions. In November 2025, the Isulu-Bushiangala project in Kakamega County was independently confirmed to hold gold reserves of 1.27 million ounces at 11.43 grams per tonne, with a gross in-situ value exceeding $5.28 billion. Bushiangala operates within this producing region, confirming the geological scale and credibility of the area from which this gold is sourced.

International scrutiny of African gold supply chains has increased markedly since 2025, raising buyer and compliance expectations for full supply chain documentation. Bushiangala operates within Kenya's regulated export framework, which means every shipment moves through official channels with the complete compliance trail that buyers, legal advisors, and financial institutions require. For buyers sourcing gold nuggets from Africa, that regulatory standing matters as much as the gold itself.

How It Works

Our export and
delivery process

As licensed gold exporters in Kenya, Bushiangala manages the full export process on the buyer's behalf. The five stages below describe how a gold nugget order moves from first enquiry to confirmed delivery.

01

Enquiry & Availability

Contact Bushiangala via the enquiry form or WhatsApp to discuss current nugget availability, lot sizes, and approximate gold content. Pricing is indicative at this stage and is confirmed once the independent assay results for your lot are known.

02

Assay & Batch Verification

The batch is independently assayed by an accredited laboratory. The assay report — confirming exact gold content, weight, and composition — is provided to the buyer for review before any agreement is signed. Pricing is calculated against the verified gold content and the prevailing LBMA spot rate.

03

Sales & Purchase Agreement

A formal purchase agreement is issued and price locks at point of signature. KYC and AML documentation is completed on both sides. The full documentation package — assay report, certificate of origin, export permits, tax compliance clearance — is compiled and provided at this stage.

04

Export Permit & Specialist Logistics

Export documentation is processed under Kenya's Mining Act. Shipment is arranged through specialist freight providers experienced in precious metals, with full transit insurance covering the value of the consignment.

05

Delivery & Confirmation

The consignment is delivered to your nominated address, refinery, or custodian with full tracking throughout. Bushiangala's export team coordinates documentation on the Kenya side; buyers are responsible for import declaration requirements in their destination country.
