What You Receive

What is included with every export shipment

Three things travel with every order before a single payment changes hands: an independent assay report confirming purity, a certificate of origin, and the export permits required for import into your destination country. That documentation arrives for review before the transaction is finalised, not after.

Bulk orders of 20 kilograms and above include a UN clearance certificate alongside the standard package. For buyers importing at that volume, it is a document that matters at both the customs stage and the compliance stage.

Bushiangala holds a Mineral Trading Licence from the State Department of Mining, a Mineral Export Permit from the Ministry of Mining, and a NEMA Environmental Certificate from the National Environment Management Authority. These are the credentials that legal advisors and compliance teams look for when assessing any licensed gold export operation in Kenya. All three are tied to this specific operation, individually verifiable, and available on request.

Direct from Source

Why buy gold directly from the mine

Physical gold from Africa can reach a buyer through many hands. Most of those hands add cost and reduce certainty about where the gold came from. Buying directly from the mine removes both problems.

The Bushiangala reserve in Kakamega County carries a verified value of $5.28 billion, with 1.27 million ounces at an average grade of 11.43 grams per tonne. A specific, named, verifiable source. For anyone looking to invest in African gold from a documented, traceable origin, that level of provenance is unusual in this market.

Buyers who choose to buy gold from Kenya directly through the operation that mined it also pay closer to spot price than through a domestic dealer. No reseller margin, because there is no reseller.
