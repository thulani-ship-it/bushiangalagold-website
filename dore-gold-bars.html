About This Product

What is a
doré gold bar?

A doré bar is gold in its closest-to-mine form. Unlike a refined investment-grade bar, which meets a defined international purity standard, doré is an alloy of gold, silver, and trace elements produced during the first stage of gold processing. It is the metal as it leaves the mine, before it undergoes final purification at a licensed refinery.

Because doré is not held to a fixed specification, it is sold on the basis of assay results. An independent laboratory analyses each batch and determines the precise gold content. That figure drives the transaction, and the buyer receives the assay report as part of their documentation package — not a generic specification sheet.

Refineries and minting facilities purchase doré as a raw material input for further processing into finished gold products. Private investors and large bullion buyers acquire it for direct ownership of physical gold at below-refined-bullion prices, closer to the economics of primary production.

How It Works

Our export and
delivery process

As licensed doré gold exporters in Kenya, Bushiangala manages the full export process on the buyer's behalf. The five stages below describe how a doré order moves from first enquiry to confirmed delivery.

01

Enquiry & Availability

Contact Bushiangala via the enquiry form or WhatsApp to discuss current doré availability, batch sizes, and approximate gold content. Pricing is indicative at this stage and is confirmed once batch assay results are known.

02

Assay & Batch Verification

The batch is independently assayed by an accredited laboratory. The assay report — confirming exact gold content, weight, and composition — is provided to the buyer for review before any agreement is signed. Pricing is calculated against the verified gold content and the prevailing LBMA spot rate.

03

Sales & Purchase Agreement

A formal purchase agreement is issued and price locks at point of signature. KYC and AML documentation is completed on both sides. The full documentation package — assay report, certificate of origin, export permits, tax compliance clearance — is compiled and provided at this stage.

04

Export Permit & Specialist Logistics

Export documentation is processed under Kenya's Mining Act. Shipment is arranged through specialist freight providers experienced in precious metals, with full transit insurance covering the value of the consignment.

05

Delivery & Confirmation

The consignment is delivered to your nominated address, refinery, or custodian with full tracking throughout. Bushiangala's export team coordinates documentation on the Kenya side; buyers are responsible for import declaration requirements in their destination country.
