Origin & Sourcing

Where the gold
comes from

The Bushiangala operation is based in Kakamega County, in the gold-bearing belt of Western Kenya. The Kakamega region is known for high-grade gold deposits, and the Isulu-Bushiangala project within it was independently confirmed in November 2025 to hold reserves of 1.27 million ounces at 11.43 grams per tonne.

All gold is extracted, refined, and sold directly by Bushiangala. Buyers are not purchasing through a dealer, an agent, or a secondary market. The supply chain runs from the Kakamega County operation to the buyer, with no intermediaries in between.

Bushiangala also holds mining operations in South Kivu, DRC and Darfur, Sudan. Details on each location are available on the dedicated mines pages.

Licences & Compliance

Credentials and
compliance

Bushiangala holds three government-issued credentials from Kenyan regulatory authorities. The Mineral Trading Licence, issued by the State Department of Mining, authorises Bushiangala to trade gold commercially within Kenya. The Mineral Export Permit, issued by the Ministry of Mining, authorises the lawful export of gold to international buyers. The NEMA Environmental Certificate, issued by the National Environment Management Authority, confirms that the operation meets Kenya's environmental standards for mining activity.

The company is registered and headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya, at 4th Floor, Room 13, Kenrail Towers, Westlands off Ring Road. The team can be reached directly on +254 100 006 445.

All three documents are individually verifiable and available to buyers on request. They are not generic industry certifications. Each one is issued by a named Kenyan government body, tied to this specific operation, and carries the information a compliance team or legal advisor needs before approving a purchase.

Every order is accompanied by an independent assay report confirming purity, a certificate of origin, and the full export documentation required for import into the buyer's destination country. Bulk orders of 20 kilograms and above additionally include a UN clearance certificate. All documentation is provided before the transaction is finalised.
